THE ITALIAN CONCERNS

From the analysis of the Italian situation, stands out that our biggest worries involve virus, spam mail, scamming, privacy issues and mature content. The chart shows the ranking between the five most important concerns and it relates them with some vital statistics: region, age and education. The maximum and minimum values are highlighted so that, not only itâ€™s shown what Italians are scared of, but also who are the most influenced ones by these worries.

VIRUS

The worry of having the computer infected by a malware program that could infect and damage personal files.

SPAM

The worry of receiving a lot of unwanted emails that have a promoting aim. This could mean spending a lot of time cleaning them.

PRIVACY

This is the concern of unintentionally sharing too much personal information on the web. For example in registering to some web services.

SCAMMING

These two are criminal activities that aim to acquire sensitive information, such as password and user id, with false emails.

18+

The Internet could be a dangerous place, especially for kids, that can possibly connect to inappropriate websites or people.

CONCERNS AND USAGE

Are they so strong to condition our usage of the Internet?

As worry is an emotion that induce people to take precautions to avoid risky behaviours, in the same way, if the concerns about the Internet are real, people more worried should be the one that use less the Internet. The chart below shows the difference between the distribution of people less - no concern or only one - and more worried - from two to five concerns, compared to the usage of the Internet. From the graph itâ€™s visible that the people that have a very low concern level are also the one that actually use less the Internet, meaning that the consciousness does not influence the usage.
