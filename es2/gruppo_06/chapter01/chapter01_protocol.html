Introduction

The graph represents the connection between three levels of See also pages in Wikipedia started from a list of 5 pages related to the research. This graph is usefull to understand which are the most important concept about file sharing of copyrighted material.

SCRAPING OF WIKIPEDIA

Selection of five pages of Wikipedia which represent the theme of the research:

  • Legal aspects of copyright infringment
  • Copyright
  • Copy protection
  • RIAA
  • File sharing

Extraction of the first levelâ€™s See Also pages and selection of which are related with the theme.

Uploading of the list in Seealsology and creation of the dataset with all the connection between them.

GRAPH

Uploading of this dataset in Gephi and creation of the graph with the Force Atlas 2 layout (LinLog mode, scaling 0.35, gravity 0.2, prevent overlap).

Filter of the nodes which have only one connection with the others.

Graphical corrections and analisys.
