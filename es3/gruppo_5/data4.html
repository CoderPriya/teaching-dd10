How does each argument and its speaker weigh?

By analyzing queries results it emerged that 72.53% of speaker line up against mass surveillance, while only 2.59% are in favour. The remaining part - 24,87% - talks about it without taking a stand.

Every speaker talks about the same arguments, but each one uses it to legitimate their own point of view.

HOW TO READ - The visualization on the left shows arguments used both against and in favour. The histogram represents the number of articles analyzed. La visualizzazione sulla destra permette di vedere sia chi è lo speaker che domina ciascun argomento contro la sorveglianza di massa, sia qual è l'argomento più discusso da ogni gruppo di speaker.
